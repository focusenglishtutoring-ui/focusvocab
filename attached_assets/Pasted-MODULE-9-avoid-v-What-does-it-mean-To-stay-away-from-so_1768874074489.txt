MODULE 9
avoid (v.) – 避免
What does it mean?
 To stay away from something to stop a problem.
Examples:
 Try to avoid junk food.
 He avoided talking about the test. (past simple)
 Do you avoid making mistakes?
Check your understanding:
 What does avoid mean?
 A) stay away from
 B) look for
 C) enjoy

awake (adj.) – 醒著的
What does it mean?
 Not sleeping.
Examples:
 She is still awake.
 I stayed awake all night.
 Are you fully awake now?
Check your understanding:
 What does awake mean?
 A) very tired
 B) not sleeping
 C) excited

award (n.) – 獎項
What does it mean?
 A prize given to someone for doing something well.
Examples:
 She won an award for her work.
 The award was given yesterday.
 Did he receive the award?
Check your understanding:
 What is an award?
 A) a test
 B) a rule
 C) a prize

award (v.) – 頒發
What does it mean?
 To give someone a prize.
Examples:
 The school awards prizes each year. (present simple –s)
 They awarded her first place. (past simple)
 Who will award the prize?
Check your understanding:
 What does award mean here?
 A) to give a prize
 B) to take something away
 C) to hide something

What does it mean?

 What is balance?

What does it mean?
 To stay steady without falling.
Examples:

Check your understanding:
 What does balance mean here?
