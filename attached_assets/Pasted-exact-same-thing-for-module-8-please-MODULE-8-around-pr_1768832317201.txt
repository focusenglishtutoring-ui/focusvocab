around (prep./adv.) – 在…周圍；大約
What does it mean?
 Near something, in a circle, or about a number or time.
Examples:
 The children sat around the table. (prep.)
 There are shops around the school. (prep.)
 There were around 20 students in the class. (adv.)
Check your understanding:
 What does around help show here?
 A) near or about
 B) far away
 C) exactly the same

arrange (v.) – 安排
What does it mean?
 To plan something or put things in order.
Examples:
 She arranges the chairs before class. (present simple –s)
 We arranged a meeting yesterday. (past simple)
 Did you arrange the trip?
Check your understanding:
 What does arrange mean?
 A) to forget
 B) to plan or put in order
 C) to break something
