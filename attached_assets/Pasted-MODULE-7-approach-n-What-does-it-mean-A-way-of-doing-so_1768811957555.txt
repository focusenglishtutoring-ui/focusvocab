MODULE 7

approach (n.) – 方法；方式
What does it mean?
 A way of doing something.
Examples:
 This is a new approach to learning English.
 Her approach to studying works well.
 What approach should we use?
Check your understanding:
 What is an approach?
 A) a way of doing something
 B) a mistake
 C) a result

approach (v.) – 接近；走近
What does it mean?
 To move closer to someone or something.
Examples:
 The dog approaches slowly. (present simple –s)
 He approached the teacher after class. (past simple)
 Did she approach the door quietly?
Check your understanding:
 What does approach mean here?
 A) to wait
 B) to move away
 C) to move closer

approve (v.) – 同意；批准
What does it mean?
 To say yes to something after thinking about it.
Examples:
 The teacher approves the plan. (present simple –s)
 The school approved the trip. (past simple)
 Did your parents approve the idea?
Check your understanding:
 What does approve mean?
 A) to say yes
 B) to forget something
 C) to ask a question

approximately (adv.) – 大約
What does it mean?
 Close to a number. It can be a little less or a little more than the real number.
Examples:
 The test takes approximately one hour.
 There were approximately 20 students.
 How many people were there approximately?
Check your understanding:
 What does approximately mean?
 A) close to a number
 B) exactly the same number
 C) very far from the number

architect (n.) – 建築師
What does it mean?
 A person who plans how buildings will look and be built.
Examples:
 The architect planned the school.
 She wants to be an architect.
 Did the architect visit the building site?
Check your understanding:
 What does an architect do?
 A) plans buildings
 B) cleans buildings
 C) sells houses

architecture (n.) – 建築；建築風格
What does it mean?
 The way buildings look and are made.
Examples:
 I like modern architecture.
 The city is famous for its architecture.
 Do you study architecture?
Check your understanding:
 What is architecture?
 A) the cost of buildings
 B) the size of buildings
 C) the way buildings look

area (n.) – 區域；地區
What does it mean?
 A part of a place.
Examples:
 This area is very quiet.
 Don’t play in that area.
 What area do you live in?
Check your understanding:
 What is an area?
 A) a person
 B) a part of a place
 C) a building

arm (n.) – 手臂
What does it mean?
 The part of the body from the shoulder to the hand.
Examples:
 He broke his arm.
 She raised her arm.
 Does your arm hurt?
Check your understanding:
 What is an arm?
 A) part of the leg
 B) part of the body
 C) part of the head

armchair (n.) – 扶手椅
What does it mean?
 A chair with arms that you sit in.
Examples:
 He sat in the armchair.
 The armchair is near the window.
 Is the armchair comfortable to sit in?
Check your understanding:
 What is an armchair?
 A) a table
 B) a chair you sit in with arms
 C) a bed

army (n.) – 陸軍；軍隊
What does it mean?
 A group of people who protect their country.
Examples:
 He joined the army last year.
 The army protects the country.
 Is the army large in this country?
Check your understanding:
 What is an army?
 A) people who teach in schools
 B) people who build roads
 C) people who protect a country
