air conditioning / air conditioner (n.) – 空調；冷氣

What does it mean?
Air conditioning means cool air in a room or building.
An air conditioner is the machine that makes the air cool.

Examples:
The air conditioning is on in the classroom.

We bought a new air conditioner for the living room.

Is the air conditioning working today?

all right / alright (adj./adv.) – 好的；沒問題

What does it mean?
Used to say something is OK or acceptable.

Examples:
The answer is all right.

It’s alright to ask for help.

Note:
“All right” and “alright” mean the same thing.
“All right” is more common in formal writing.
