MODULE 10

bank (n.) – 銀行
What does it mean?
 A place where people keep money or get money.
Examples:
 I need to go to the bank today.
 She works at a bank.
 Is there a bank near here?
Check your understanding:
 What is a bank?
 A) a place for money
 B) a hospital
 C) a park

base (n.) – 基礎；底部
What does it mean?
 The bottom part of something, or the main part it stands on.
Examples:
 The statue has a wide base.
 This chair has a strong base.
 Is the base strong enough?
Check your understanding:
 What is a base?
 A) the top part
 B) the bottom part
 C) the color

base (v.) – 以…為根據
What does it mean?
 To use something as the main idea or reason.
Examples:
 The story is based on real events. (past simple)
 We base our answer on the text.
 Is this idea based on facts?
Check your understanding:
 What does base mean here?
 A) to throw away
 B) to hide
 C) to use as the main idea

basic (adj.) – 基本的
What does it mean?
 Simple and most important.
Examples:
 We learned some basic words.
 This is basic math.
 Do you know the basic rules?
Check your understanding:
 What does basic mean?
 A) very difficult
 B) simple and important
 C) dangerous

battle (n.) – 戰爭；戰鬥
What does it mean?
 A fight between groups of people.
Examples:
 The battle lasted all day.
 They talked about the famous battle.
 Who won the battle?
Check your understanding:
 What is a battle?
 A) a game
 B) a lesson
 C) a fight

battle (v.) – 奮鬥；對抗
What does it mean?
 To try very hard to deal with a problem.
Examples:
 He battles his fears. (present simple –s)
 She battled to finish the work. (past simple)
 Are they battling the problem together?
Check your understanding:
 What does battle mean here?
 A) to rest
 B) to try very hard
 C) to forget

bean (n.) – 豆子
What does it mean?
 A small round food from a plant.
Examples:
 She cooked some beans.
 These beans are tasty.
 Do you like beans?
Check your understanding:
 What is a bean?
 A) a drink
 B) a tool
 C) a kind of food

bear (v.) – 忍受；承受
What does it mean?
 To accept or deal with something difficult.
Examples:
 I can’t bear the noise.
 She bore the pain quietly. (past simple)
 Can you bear waiting a little longer?
Check your understanding:
 What does bear mean here?
 A) to accept or deal with
 B) to run fast
 C) to shout

beat (v.) – 打敗；敲打
What does it mean?
 To win against someone, or hit something again and again.
Examples:
 Our team beat theirs. (past simple)
 He beats the drum loudly. (present simple –s)
 Did you beat the game?
Check your understanding:
 What does beat mean here?
 A) to lose
 B) to win or hit
 C) to stop

beauty (n.) – 美；美麗
What does it mean?
 Something that looks very nice or makes people feel good when they see it.
Examples:
 The beauty of the place surprised us.
 She talked about the beauty of nature.
 Do you see the beauty in simple things?
Check your understanding:
 What is beauty?
 A) something very loud
 B) something dangerous
 C) something that looks very nice
